# Deformation Rules

Deformation is disabled by default and only appears when the manifest mode, `structure_type`, and `anchor_type` match an allowed rule.

- `paper-funnel-body`: gently becomes a paper-funnel shape for filtering metaphors.
- `inbox-nest`: sits inside a small inbox-like bottleneck.
- `card-stack-squish`: gently squished by a stack of task cards.
