# Action Library

- `slump-carry`: slumps forward while carrying raw material.
- `limp-pull`: reluctantly pulls a lever or rope connected to the mechanism.
- `sleepy-sort`: sorts blocks or tokens with tired deadpan focus.
- `soft-point`: points at a key comparison while half-slumped over the workbench.
- `droop-repair`: droops over a tiny tool while repairing a low-tech machine.
