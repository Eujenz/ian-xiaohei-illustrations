# Visual System

Use 16:9 horizontal article illustrations with mostly white background, black hand-drawn line art, low-tech physical metaphors, lots of whitespace, and light accent colors only when needed.

The result is not PPT, not infographic, not UI, and not vector corporate art.

Do not render readable Chinese text. Include blank placeholders, blank labels, blank signs, blank callouts, or blank notes where deterministic overlay labels will be added later.
