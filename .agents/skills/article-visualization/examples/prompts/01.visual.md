## 1. Canvas spec
16:9 horizontal article illustration, 1536x864 canvas, mostly white background.

## 2. Scene concept
Theme: judgment from noisy material. Core idea: filtering noisy material into useful judgment. Use a low-tech physical metaphor: a low-tech funnel sorting messy scraps into one clear block.

## 3. Character block
small solid rounded square body, two tiny white dot eyes, two thin legs, slightly shaky black hand-drawn line, serious quiet slightly absurd worker energy. Role: A working participant inside the concept, never a decorative mascot.. Avoid: no complex clothing; no mascot poses; no corner decoration; no children's cartoon style; no full-color corporate mascot treatment; no unrecognizable deformation. Action: The character carries a heavy bundle of mixed paper scraps into the scene.

## 4. Conditional deformation block
Conditionally deform the square worker into a simple recognizable funnel-like body while keeping the two white dot eyes and thin legs.

## 5. Text strategy block
Do not render readable Chinese text. Leave blank placeholders for labels that will be added later by overlay_text.py.

## 6. Blank label placeholder block

- label_02: blank callout beside the clear output block; leave it blank for later overlay.

## 7. Style constraints
Hand-drawn black line art, slightly shaky ink line, mostly white background, lots of whitespace, light accent colors only when needed.

## 8. Negative constraints
No readable Chinese text, no sticker phrases, no PPT style, no infographic UI, no vector corporate art, no decorative mascot posing.
