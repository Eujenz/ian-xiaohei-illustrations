## 1. Canvas spec
16:9 horizontal article illustration, 1536x864 canvas, mostly white background.

## 2. Scene concept
Theme: 系統瓶頸：文字渲染是一台卡住的機器. Core idea: 真正的瓶頸不是畫不出圖，而是模型常把短標籤渲染成錯字。這個系統局部需要被隔離：圖像模型只負責低科技隱喻和留白，文字渲染交給 deterministic renderer。. Use a low-tech physical metaphor: two worktables comparing unstable in-image text with stable overlay labels.

## 3. Character block
small solid rounded square body, two tiny white dot eyes, two thin legs, slightly shaky black hand-drawn line, serious quiet slightly absurd worker energy. Role: A working participant inside the concept, never a decorative mascot.. Avoid: no complex clothing; no mascot poses; no corner decoration; no children's cartoon style; no full-color corporate mascot treatment; no unrecognizable deformation. Action: The character repairs a patched low-tech machine with a tiny wrench.

## 4. Conditional deformation block
No character deformation for this shot.

## 5. Text strategy block
Do not render readable Chinese text. Leave blank placeholders for labels that will be added later by overlay_text.py.

## 6. Blank label placeholder block
- label_01: blank note near the primary visual anchor; leave it blank for later overlay.
- label_02: blank callout near the secondary visual anchor; leave it blank for later overlay.

## 7. Style constraints
Hand-drawn black line art, slightly shaky ink line, mostly white background, lots of whitespace, light accent colors only when needed.

## 8. Negative constraints
No readable Chinese text, no sticker phrases, no PPT style, no infographic UI, no vector corporate art, no decorative mascot posing.
